First Post

It’s been a long time since I had a format for sharing thoughts in long form; not since LiveJournal days.

I miss it. I miss the luxury of words that haven’t been trimmed to unnatural shortness for the sole purpose of maximizing likability. I’m tired of working for free, sharing my integrity and authenticity for an abusive corporation’s profit.

Watching a friend resume his own practice has inspired me to resume a long-form discipline of my own.

And so here we are.
